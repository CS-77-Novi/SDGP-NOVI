The Novi Advantage: Advanced ML Detection & Intervention

Core Detection Metrics

  • **Eye Features:** Track **Eye Status** and critical indicators like **Gaze direction / fixation duration** (looking away).
  • **Head & Posture:** Detect signs of disengagement through **Head pose** (e.g., frequent looking down or slumping). **Body posture** can also be assessed via pose estimation (if camera shows upper body).
  • **Facial Expressions:** Identify signs of fatigue and boredom using **Yawning detection** and specific **Facial Action Units (AU)**.

Real-Time Re-Engagement Tools

  • **Pop-up Question:** When a student is distracted for a prolonged period, questions uploaded by the host/teacher pop up to test interest and re-engage them.
  • **A Mini-Game:** Automated prompts can launch a quick, study-related mini-game, like re-arranging letters of a word related to the study material.
  • **Kids Feature:** A separate, engaging **UI interface design** specifically tailored to minimize distraction for younger learners.

Intelligent Learning Aids

Rewind/Playback

During a live meeting, if a student didn’t understand a certain part, they can privately **go back 2-3 minutes and rewatch** the content, then seamlessly return to the live session.

Summarizer/Transcript

By analyzing the host’s voice, the platform generates a **full transcript** in real-time, followed by an **automated summary** for quick, post-lecture review.
